    AutoCare Services

    These are the services we provide

    NB!! All prices include standard labour unless stated otherwise. Final quotes may vary based on vehicle type and vehicle condition and additional parts required.


    Engine & Mechanical Repairs:

    Fix performance issuse and keep your engine in top condition

    Service include:

    
                        - Engine diagnostics & repairs
                        - Transmission repairs
                        - Suspension & steering repairs
                        - Exhaust system repairs

    Estimated Pricing:

                            - Minor Repairs: From R5,000
                        - Moderate Repairs: R10,000 – R20,000+
                        - Major Engine Repairs/Overhaul: R16,000 – R50,000+

    Routine Maintenance:

    Keep your car running smoothly with regular servicing

    Services include:

                            - Oil change & oil filter replacement
                        - Air filter replacement
                        - Fluid checks & top-ups (coolant, brake fluid, etc.)
                        - Spark plug replacement
                        - General vehicle inspection

    Estimated Pricing:

                            - Minor Service: R1,000 – R3,500
                        - Major Service: R2,500 – R8,000+

    Tyre & Wheel Services:

    Improve safety, handling and tyre lifespan:

    Service include:

                            - Wheel alignment
                        - Wheel balancing
                        - Tyre rotation
                        - Tyre fitting & replacement

    Estimated Pricing

                            - Wheel Alignment: R200 – R500
                        - Wheel Balancing: R50 – R100 per wheel
                        - Tyre Rotation: R100 – R300
                        - Tyre Fitment: R80 – R200 per tyre

    Electrical & Diagnostics:

    Identify and fix Electrical and system faults

    Services include:

                            - Battery testing & replacement
                        - Alternator repairs
                        - Starter motor repairs
                        - Computer diagnostics (check engine light)
                        - Wiring & lighting repairs

    Estimated Pricing

                            - Diagnostics: R300 – R800
                        - Battery Replacement: R800 – R2,500
                        - Electrical Repairs: R1,000 – R5,000+

    Brake Service:

    Ensure your safety with reliable braking system

    Service include:

                            - Brake pad replacement
                        - Brake disc/rotor repair or replacement
                        - Brake fluid checks & replacement

    Estimated Pricing:

                            - Brake Pads: R800 – R2,500
                        - Disc/Rotors: R1,500 – R4,000+
                        - Full Brake Service: R2,000 – R6,000

    Cooling & Air Conditioning:

    Stay cool and prevent engine overheating

    Service include:

                            - Radiator repairs
                        - Coolant system service
                        - Air conditioning regas & repairs

    Estimated Pricing:

                            - Coolant Service: R500 – R1,500
                        - Radiator Repairs: R1,000 – R4,000+
                        - Aircon Service: R800 – R2,500

    Body & Paint Services:

    Restore your vehicle

    Services include:

                            - Dent removal
                        - Scratch repair
                        - Panel beating
                        - Full or partial respray

    Estimated Pricing

                            - Minor Scratch Repair: R500 – R2,000
                        - Dent Repair: R1,000 – R5,000
                        - Full Respray: R10,000 – R30,000+

    Additional Services:

    Extra services to enhance your vehicle's condition and comfort

    Services include:

                            - Car wash & detailing
                        - Windoscreen repair/replacement
                        - Vehicle inspection

    Estimated Pricing

                            - Car Wash: R100 - R300
                        - Detailing: R500 - R2,000+
                        - Windoscreen Replacement: R1,500 - R5,000
