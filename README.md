WEBSITE PROPOSAL: AUTOCARE SERVICE
1. Organisation Overview
Name: AutoCare Service
Brief History:
AutoCare Service is a start-up concept designed to address inefficiencies in traditional vehicle servicing systems. The idea emerged from the observation that many local garages still rely on outdated booking methods such as phone calls and walk-ins, creating inconvenience for customers. The business aims to modernize this process through a digital solution.
Mission Statement:
To provide a convenient, transparent, and accessible platform for vehicle servicing through digital innovation.
Vision Statement:
To become a leading online platform for vehicle maintenance bookings, improving customer experience and operational efficiency in the automotive service industry.
Target Audience:
•	Car owners in urban areas 
•	Working professionals who prefer convenience 
•	Young, tech-savvy drivers 
•	Customers seeking transparent pricing and quick service

1. Market Problem
Briefly describe the pain point or unmet need in the market. Include stats, user quotes, or trends that validate the gap.
Many car owners struggle to find reliable and convenient vehicle servicing options. Most local garages still rely on phone calls or walk ins to schedule services, which can be inconvenient and time consuming for customers.
Customers often do not know:
•	What services their car needs
•	How much the service will cost
•	When the garages is available 
This lack of transparency and online booking options create frustration for customers and inefficiency for service providers.
There is a growing demand for digital platforms that simplify booking and managing vehicles maintenance services.
